1. Scope

This Privacy Policy (“Policy”) explains how Miru Coffee (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use or interact with our website (the “Site”) and related services (collectively, “Services”).

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.
