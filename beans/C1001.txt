<!-- Producer Notes -->
<h4>Producer Notes</h4>
<div class="producer-notes">
  <p>
    Cocatel is one of the largest cooperatives in Brazil with nearly 7,000 members. With five years of collaboration, our Luzia Blend reflects their dedication to sustainability and traceability.
  </p>
  <p>
    The cooperative was founded in 1961 and is headquartered in Tres Pontas, Minas Gerais. Inventory management is fully integrated with the cooperative’s quality control lab to ensure traceability and consistency.
  </p>
  <p>
    Their direct-trade specialty division focuses on microlots and blends that reflect the best of Minas Gerais. All coffees are grown around the town of Tres Pontas and selected by their expert quality team.
  </p>
</div>
