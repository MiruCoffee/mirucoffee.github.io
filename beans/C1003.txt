<h4>Producer Notes</h4>
<div class="producer-notes">
  <p>
    This lot comes from over 500 smallholder farmers near Gigesa in the Guji Zone. Cherries are hand-picked at peak ripeness,
    then dried slowly on raised beds at high altitude for 12–15 days. 
  </p>
  <p>
    Thanks to the natural processing method and the highland terroir of Shakiso, the resulting coffee is vibrant and complex,
    showcasing layers of red fruit, floral aromatics, and velvety body.
  </p>
