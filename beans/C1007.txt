<h4>Producer Notes</h4>
<div class="producer-notes">
  <p>
    The Jauja Blend from El Pesadero was created by the Coto family.
  </p>
  <p>
    El Pesadero is centrally located in the municipality of Antigua. Antigua is surrounded by 3 volcanoes, Volcan de Agua, Volcan de Acatenango, and Volcan de Fuego. The rich volcanic soil, warm days and cool nights and high winds give Antigua its unique microclimate that has been highly sought after for many years and provides perfect conditions for processing coffees.
  </p>
