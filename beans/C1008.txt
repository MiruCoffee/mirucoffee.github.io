<!-- Producer Notes -->
<h4>Producer Notes</h4>
<div class="producer-notes">
  <p>
    This coffee comes from Costa Rica’s Tarrazú region and is processed by Beneficio San Diego,
    one of Costa Rica’s long-established coffee mills. Founded in 1888, the mill works with producers
    from the Tarrazú and Tres Ríos regions, with a strong focus on quality, processing efficiency,
    and sustainable production practices.
  </p>
  <p>
    The honey process is closely associated with Costa Rica. In this method, the coffee cherry is depulped,
    but part of the sticky mucilage remains on the parchment during drying. This allows natural sugars to
    contribute sweetness and a rounder cup profile. For this lot, ripe cherries are carefully selected,
    depulped, and dried slowly on African beds or patios to preserve clarity, sweetness, and balance.
  </p>
</div>
