Welcome to Miru Coffee

Air-roasted specialty coffee, handcrafted in small batches from rural Texas.

At Miru Coffee, we use a modern air roaster to precisely roast premium-grade beans. Our method produces a cleaner, more vibrant flavor — no smokiness, just the pure expression of origin.

We roast fresh every two weeks. You can buy one bag or subscribe to receive a rotating selection every 2, 4, or 6 weeks.
