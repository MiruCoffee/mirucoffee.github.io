About Miru Coffee

At Miru Coffee, we are passionate about flavor and craftsmanship. We select only premium specialty-grade beans from trusted producers across the globe. Using our air roasting method, we unlock clean, vibrant flavors without smokiness.

Every roast is crafted in small batches, typically every two weeks, and shipped within two days to ensure freshness. Our roasting style is guided by curiosity, sensory testing, and a deep respect for origin and process.

Based in rural Texas, we proudly serve neighbors near and far who care about quality, transparency, and good coffee.
